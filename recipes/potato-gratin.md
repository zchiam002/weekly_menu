# Potato Gratin (Pommes Anna Style)

> Thinly sliced potatoes layered with deeply caramelised onions, clarified butter and beef stock, pressed and slow-baked into tender layers with golden, crispy edges and fresh thyme.

**Servings:** To taste (layered bake)

## Ingredients

- Agria potatoes
- 1 block butter
- Brown onions
- Beef stock
- Oil
- Salt, pepper, fresh thyme

## Method

1. **Caramelise the onions:** Salt the sliced onions to draw out moisture. Cook gently over medium heat until deeply caramelised, 30–40 min. Avoid any black bits (bitterness).
2. **Prepare the potatoes:** Thinly slice on a mandolin for uniform slices. Do **not** wash after slicing — keep the starch so the layers bind.
3. **Clarify the butter:** Melt butter in the microwave ~20 sec. Milk solids can stay in — they won't burn at this temperature.
4. **Layer:** In an oven-safe pan, spread caramelised onions, then a thin layer of potato. Drizzle clarified butter and beef stock; season with salt and pepper. Alternate the slice direction each layer. Continue layering potato, butter, stock and seasoning.
5. **Thyme:** After the last potato layer, season again and sprinkle fresh thyme.
6. **Cartouche:** Cut greaseproof paper to fit with a small steam hole in the centre; lay on top.
7. **Bake:** Weigh down with a heavy plate. Bake at 120°C (250°F) for 1–1.5 hr. Halfway, flip the plate for even pressure.
8. **Increase heat:** After 45 min–1 hr, remove cartouche and plate. Raise to 130°C (265°F) to reduce the stock and caramelise the top — ~25 min more, until golden and tender.
9. **Glaze:** In the last 5 min, brush the top with more clarified butter and a little beef stock for a shiny glaze. Sprinkle more thyme.
10. **Serve:** Rest a few minutes; serve with crispy edges and tender layers.
