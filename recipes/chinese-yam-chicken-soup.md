# Chinese Yam & Chicken Soup 山药鸡汤

> A clear, aromatic and nourishing soup of Chinese yam (山药) with chicken, dried scallops, red dates and goji berries.

**Servings:** 5–6 (½ chicken + 2L water)

## Ingredients

- ½ chicken (半只鸡)
- 350g Chinese yam (山药), peeled and cut into chunks
- 3 dried scallops (干贝)
- 8 red dates (红枣)
- 20g goji berries (枸杞子)
- 2 tbsp cooking wine (料酒)
- 2L water
- Salt, to taste
