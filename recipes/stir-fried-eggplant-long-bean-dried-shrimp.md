# Stir-Fried Eggplant & Long Bean with Dried Shrimp 虾米炒三宝

> Restaurant-grade stir-fried "three treasures" — deep-fried eggplant with okra and long beans, tossed with fragrant dried shrimp.

**Servings:** 3–4

## Ingredients

- 420g vegetables (285g eggplant, 66g lady's fingers/okra, 75g long beans)
  - Coat the eggplant with ~2 tbsp cornflour before deep-frying
- 30g dried shrimp (soak in 60ml water to soften, then chop — keep the soaking liquid for the dish; deep-fry half, use the rest when cooking)
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- ½ tbsp oyster sauce
- ¼ tsp sugar
