# Sambal Kangkong 参巴空心菜

> Singapore zi char-style stir-fried morning glory with rempah chilli paste.

**Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

### Rempah paste (chilli paste)
- 2 fresh red chillies, deseeded and roughly chopped
- 4 dried chillies, soaked and deseeded
- 2 bird's-eye chillies, deseeded
- 1 medium red onion, cut into wedges
- 4 garlic cloves, peeled
- 10g dried shrimp, soaked in 4 tbsp water (reserve soaking water for the paste)
- 1–2 tbsp cooking oil (to help with blending)
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar

### Other
- 220g kangkong (morning glory / water spinach)
- 2 tbsp water
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
