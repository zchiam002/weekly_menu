# Eggplant with Minced Pork

> Super Easy Braised Eggplant in Garlic Sauce w/ Minced Meat — 鱼香茄子

**Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

- 320g eggplant (cut into chunks, soak in saltwater for a few mins)
- 200g minced pork (or chicken or beef)
- 1 thumb-sized piece young ginger, chopped
- 5 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 stalk spring onion, chopped (add stem first, green part at the end)
- 1 tbsp spicy broad bean paste 辣豆瓣酱
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1 tbsp premium dark soy sauce
- 0.5 tsp sugar
- 80ml water
- 1 tbsp black vinegar
- Some cornstarch solution
