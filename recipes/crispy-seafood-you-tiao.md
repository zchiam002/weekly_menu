# Crispy Seafood You Tiao 海鲜油条

> Youtiao (Chinese fried dough sticks) stuffed with a bouncy squid-and-prawn paste, fried crisp and finished with toasted sesame seeds.

**Servings:** 4–5 (in a multi-dish meal)

## Ingredients

### Seafood paste
- 140g (4.9 oz) squid body, cut into small pieces (approx. 240g / 8.5 oz including head — reserve head for another dish)
- 120g (4.2 oz) peeled and deveined prawns (approx. 230g / 8.1 oz with shells)
- 1 tsp salt
- A few dashes white pepper
- 2 tbsp plain flour
- 1 egg white

### To assemble
- 2 youtiao (Chinese fried dough sticks)
- Toasted sesame seeds
