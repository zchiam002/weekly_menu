# Kimchi Pancake (Kimchijeon)

> Korean kimchi pancake — hot, spicy, crispy and a little sour. One big 12-inch pancake from well-fermented kimchi, scallions and a simple flour batter. Maangchi's recipe.

**Servings:** 1 large 12-inch pancake

## Ingredients

- ½ lb well-fermented napa cabbage kimchi, chopped small, plus 2 tbsp of the brine
- 3 scallions, chopped
- ½ tsp sugar
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- ½ cup water
- 4 tbsp vegetable oil

## Method

1. **Batter:** Combine the kimchi, kimchi brine, scallions, sugar, flour and water in a medium bowl and mix well with a spoon.
2. **Heat:** Heat a 12-inch non-stick skillet over medium heat. (No 12-inch pan? Use a smaller one and make 2 pancakes.) Add 2 tbsp of the oil and swirl to coat the base.
3. **Spread:** Pour in the batter and spread with the back of a spoon or spatula into a large circle.
4. **First side:** Cook until the bottom is golden brown and crisp, 3–5 min.
5. **Flip & oil:** Carefully turn the pancake over. Drizzle the remaining 2 tbsp oil around the edges, lift the pancake with a thin spatula so the oil runs underneath, and tilt the pan to spread it evenly.
6. **Second side:** Cook until the bottom is light golden brown and crisp, 3–5 min.
7. **Finish:** Flip once more and cook another minute. Slide onto a platter and serve immediately.

> For maximum crisp: use a generous amount of oil and make sure it gets under the pancake, use a non-stick pan, and follow the ingredient ratios closely.
