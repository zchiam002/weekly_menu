# Shimeji Spinach Tofu 菠菜豆腐扒蘑菇

> Homemade spinach egg tofu — steamed, set, then deep-fried golden — laid over poached spinach and topped with shimeji mushrooms in a savoury gravy.

**Servings:** 6

## Ingredients

### Tofu (steam 25 min, cool 2 hrs, then deep-fry until golden)
- ½L unsweetened soya bean milk
- 7 eggs
- ½ tsp salt
- 100g spinach, finely chopped & squeezed dry

### Frying mushrooms
- 1–2 tbsp cooking oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 100g shimeji mushrooms

### Gravy
- 500ml water
- ½ chicken stock cube
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- ½ tsp sugar
- ½ tbsp dark soy sauce
- ¼ tsp salt
- Some cornstarch solution (cornflour + water), to thicken
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp Chinese cooking wine (Shaoxing Huatiao)

### Vegetable base
- Spinach, poached with a little oil and salt — lay on the serving plate, then place the tofu on top
