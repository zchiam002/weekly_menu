# Taiwanese XXL Fried Chicken 台湾超大鸡扒

> Giant crunchy Taiwanese fried chicken cutlet — butterflied chicken breast marinated with five-spice and ginger, coated in coarse sweet potato flour for maximum crunch.

**Servings:** 3–4

## Ingredients

### Marinated chicken
- 3 tbsp water
- 3 tbsp Chinese white rice wine or sake
- ½ tsp salt
- 1½ tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp young ginger juice
- 3 tbsp light soy sauce
- 3 cloves garlic, grated
- 1 tsp five-spice powder
- A few dashes white pepper
- ½ tbsp cornflour (cornstarch)
- 3 chicken breasts (approx. 770g / 27.1 oz total), butterflied and flattened

### Spice mix
- 2 tsp five-spice powder
- 1 tsp salt

### Batter coating
- 140g (4.9 oz) special grade coarse sweet potato flour
- 15g (0.5 oz) glutinous rice flour
