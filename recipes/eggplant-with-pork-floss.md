# Crispy Eggplant with Pork Floss 肉松脆茄子

> Restaurant-style crispy battered eggplant tossed in Japanese mayo and topped with pork floss and spring onions.

**Servings:** 8–10

## Ingredients

### Batter
- 150g (5.29 oz) plain flour / all-purpose flour
- 70g (2.47 oz) cornflour (cornstarch) / tapioca flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp chicken stock powder
- A few dashes white pepper
- ½ tsp baking soda
- 260ml (8.79 fl oz) water
- 1 tbsp cooking oil

### Eggplant / brinjal
- 380g (13.4 oz) eggplant (brinjal), peeled and cut slightly thicker than a finger; soak in salted water to prevent oxidation; pat dry before coating

### Other
- 2–3 tbsp Japanese mayonnaise (or until all eggplant is coated)
- 30–50g (1.06–1.76 oz) pork floss (or chicken floss)
- Some spring onions, chopped (optional)
