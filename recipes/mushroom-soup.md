# Cream of Mushroom Soup

> Full-flavoured homemade cream of mushroom soup with garlic, onions, thyme, and Marsala. Rich, earthy, comforting.

**Servings:** 6

## Ingredients

### Base
- 4 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp oil
- 2 cups onions, diced (about 2 medium)
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1½ pounds brown / cremini mushrooms, fresh, sliced
- 4 tsp chopped thyme (divided)
- ½ cup Marsala wine (or any dry red or white — Sherry, Merlot, Pinot Noir, Pinot Grigio, Sauv Blanc)

### Soup
- 6 tbsp all-purpose flour (or 2–3 tbsp cornstarch slurried with ¼ cup water)
- 4 cups low-sodium chicken broth or stock
- 1–2 tsp salt, to taste
- ½–1 tsp cracked black pepper, to taste
- 2 beef bouillon cubes, crumbled
- 1 cup heavy cream or half-and-half (sub evaporated milk if needed)

### Garnish
- ½ tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- ½ tbsp fresh thyme, chopped

## Method

1. Heat butter and oil in a large pot over medium-high heat until melted. Sauté onion 2–3 minutes until softened. Add garlic and cook ~1 minute until fragrant.
2. Add mushrooms and 2 tsp thyme; cook 5 minutes. Pour in wine and cook 3 minutes.
3. Sprinkle flour over mushrooms, stir well, cook 2 minutes. Add stock, stir, bring to a boil. Reduce to low-medium, season with salt, pepper, and crumbled bouillon cubes.
4. Cover and simmer 10–15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thickened.
5. Reduce heat to low. Stir in cream or half-and-half. Gently simmer (do not boil). Adjust salt and pepper.
6. Stir in parsley and remaining thyme. Serve warm.

### Notes
- Dairy-free: leave out the cream entirely — it's still creamy from the base.
- Stock swap: beef stock gives deeper flavour but can be too rich for some.
