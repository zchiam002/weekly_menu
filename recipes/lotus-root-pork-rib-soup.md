# Lotus Root & Pork Rib Soup 莲藕排骨汤

> Hubei-style nourishing soup with pork ribs, lotus root, ginger, and optional seaweed and goji berries — simmered low and slow for at least 4 hours.

**Servings:** 12

## Ingredients

- 900g (2 lb) pork ribs or pork neck bones
- 450g (1 lb) lotus root, peeled and cut into large chunks just before cooking
- 4 slices ginger
- 12 cups cold water
- Salt, to taste
- 1 scallion, finely chopped (to garnish)

### Optional
- 1 cup rehydrated seaweed / kelp (kombu) — avoid seaweed "knots" as they can be sandy
- 1 tbsp dried goji berries
