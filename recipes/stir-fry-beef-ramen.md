# Stir-Fry Beef Ramen (Ginger & Spring Onion)

> Tender velveted beef stir-fried with ginger, red onion and spring onion, tossed through softened instant ramen noodles in a savoury oyster-soy-fish sauce.

**Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

### Marinated beef
- 300g (10.6 oz) beef, thinly sliced (an inexpensive cut like chuck tender or frying steak)
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- ½ tsp sugar
- Less than ½ tsp baking soda (bicarbonate of soda)
- 1 tbsp cornflour
- A few dashes ground white pepper
- 2 tbsp water
- 1–2 tbsp cooking oil

### Seasoning sauce
- 200ml (7.0 fl oz) water
- 1½ tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp premium dark soy sauce (a little more for deeper colour)
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
- ½ tsp sugar
- A few dashes ground white pepper
- ½ tbsp sesame oil

### Main
- 4 blocks instant noodles, softened in hot water and drained
- 15g (0.5 oz) young ginger, sliced
- ½ red onion, sliced
- 4–5 spring onions, cut into 5cm (2-inch) lengths
- 4–5 garlic cloves, chopped
