# Sticky Pork Belly 红烧肉

> Grandma's old-school Chinese sticky braised pork belly. Rock sugar caramel base, soy braise, no alcohol — just deep savoury-sweet glaze on tender pork.

**Servings:** 4–6

## Ingredients

- 420g pork belly, cut into cubes
- 5 slices ginger (young or old)
- 50g rock sugar
- Hot water, enough to cover the pork
- 3 cloves garlic, lightly smashed with skin on
- 6 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1 tbsp premium dark soy sauce
- Cornstarch solution (optional, for finishing)
