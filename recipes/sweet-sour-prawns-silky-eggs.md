# Sweet & Sour Prawns on Silky Eggs 酸甜虾仁滑蛋

> Prawns in a quick sweet-and-sour ketchup sauce spooned over soft silky scrambled eggs.

**Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

### Marinated prawns
- 8 prawns, deshelled & deveined (325g / 11.5 oz shell-on, or 180g / 6.3 oz shelled)
- ⅓ tsp salt
- A few dashes ground white pepper

### Eggs
- 6 medium eggs
- Cornflour solution (20ml / 0.7 fl oz water + ½ tsp cornflour)
- ½ tsp salt
- A few dashes ground white pepper

### Sauce
- 125ml (4.4 fl oz) water
- ½ tbsp cornflour
- 3 tbsp tomato ketchup
- 1 tbsp vinegar
- ½ tsp salt
- 1½ tsp sugar
- 2–3 cloves garlic, chopped

### To garnish
- A handful finely sliced spring onions
