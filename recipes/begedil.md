# Begedil

> Malay potato patties — fried potato mashed with spring onion, fried shallots and spice, shaped into patties, egg-coated and deep-fried golden. Great with soto, mee soto or lontong.

**Servings:** 8 pieces | **Prep:** 10 minutes

## Ingredients

- 5–6 medium potatoes, sliced into 1 cm thick wedges
- Groundnut (peanut) oil, for frying
- 1 stalk spring onion, chopped
- 2 tbsp fried shallots
- 1 tsp white pepper powder
- ¼ tsp cumin powder
- ½ tbsp salt
- 1 egg, beaten (for the mix)
- 2 eggs, beaten (for coating)

## Method

1. **Fry & mash:** Fry the sliced potatoes on low heat until soft and light golden brown. Drain, then mash in a bowl. Add the spring onion, fried shallots, white pepper, cumin powder, the 1 beaten egg and salt. Mix well.
2. **Shape:** Knead the mixture by hand and roll into large round balls. Press to flatten slightly into patties.
3. **Heat oil:** Heat oil over medium-high heat for frying.
4. **Coat & fry:** Coat each patty in the beaten egg and put straight into the oil. They will float to the surface when ready. Fry until deep golden brown.
5. **Serve:** Drain and cool on a wire rack; serve hot.
