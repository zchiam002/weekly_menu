# Baked Salmon with Fresh Herbs

> Light, flaky baked salmon topped with a fresh herb, garlic, and olive oil paste. 25 minutes start to finish.

**Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

- 20oz (~570g) salmon fillet, ideally centre-cut, skin-on
- 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, grated
- 1 cup fresh herbs, minced (e.g. dill, parsley, thyme, oregano)
- 1 tsp salt
- ½ tsp pepper

## Method

1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C). Spray a baking sheet with non-stick spray, or line with foil and spray.
2. Place salmon skin-side down on the sheet.
3. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, grated garlic, minced herbs, salt, and pepper.
4. Spread the herb mixture evenly over the salmon with a spoon or pastry brush.
5. Bake 12–15 minutes, until fork-tender and the thickest part reads 145°F (63°C) internal.
6. Rest a few minutes before serving. Garnish with extra fresh herbs or a squeeze of lemon if you like.

### Notes
- Skin on locks in moisture during baking; remove before serving if preferred.
- Wild-caught Alaskan salmon recommended when available.
- Leftovers taste excellent cold the next day — good on salads.
