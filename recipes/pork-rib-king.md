# Pork Rib King

**Servings:** 3–4

## Ingredients

### Marinade
- 600g pork chop or pork ribs
- 1.5 tsp baking soda (can be reduced to 0.5 tsp)
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 0.5 tsp salt
- 0.5 tsp sugar
- 0.5 tsp five spice powder
- 0.5 tsp curry powder
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1 tbsp Shaoxing Huatiao cooking wine
- 1 egg
- 3 tbsp cornstarch
- A few dashes of pepper

### Tangy gravy
- 2 tbsp plum sauce
- 1 tbsp chilli sauce
- 2 tbsp ketchup
- 2 tbsp A1 sauce
- 2 tbsp HP sauce
- 1 tbsp sugar
- Some cornstarch solution
- 2–3 cloves garlic
- 1 cup water
