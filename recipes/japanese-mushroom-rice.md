# Japanese Mushroom Rice きのこご飯

> Umami-rich takikomi gohan with a mix of Japanese mushrooms cooked in a dashi-soy broth, finished with butter, chives, and sea salt flakes.

**Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

### Rice & mushrooms
- 2 rice cooker cups (360ml / 300g) uncooked Japanese short-grain white rice
- 200g (7 oz) assortment of Japanese mushrooms — e.g. ½ pack (50g) shimeji, 2 small king oyster (eringi), and 5 shiitake

### Rice seasoning
- 1¼ cups dashi (use vegan dashi for vegetarian/vegan)
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp mirin
- 2 tbsp sake
- ½ tsp Diamond Crystal kosher salt
- 1 knob ginger, grated (~½ tsp for 4 servings)
- 1 garlic clove, minced

### Toppings
- 2 tbsp unsalted butter, divided
- 5 stalks chives (or scallions / green onions), finely chopped
- ½ tsp sea salt flakes
