# Chicken Noodle Soup

> The Best Chicken Noodle Soup — Inspired Taste (Joanne Gallagher). Tender chicken thighs, simple veg, deeply flavoured broth in under 40 minutes.

**Servings:** 6
**Total time:** 40 min (5 prep · 35 cook)

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp (28g) butter
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 2 large carrots, chopped
- 2 ribs celery, chopped
- 1 heaping tbsp minced garlic (~4 cloves)
- 2 bay leaves
- 3 sprigs fresh thyme (or ½ tsp dried thyme)
- 8 cups (1.89L) low-sodium chicken stock (homemade preferred)
- Fine sea salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
- 1 lb (450g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs (4–5 thighs)
- 5 oz (140g) egg noodles (or pasta of choice)
- ¼ cup fresh parsley, finely chopped

## Directions

1. **Cook the vegetables.** Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion, carrots, celery — cook ~5 min until onions translucent and veg softened (don't brown). Stir in garlic, bay leaves, thyme; cook 30 sec–1 min until fragrant.
2. **Make the broth.** Pour in chicken stock; bring to a gentle simmer. Taste and season with salt and pepper *now* — store-bought broths are often under-seasoned, and seasoning early builds depth.
3. **Poach the chicken.** Nestle thighs into the pot so they're submerged. Return to a low simmer, partially cover (leave a small gap for steam). Cook ~20 min until tender.
4. **Cook the noodles.** Lift chicken onto a plate. Stir noodles into the simmering soup. Cook 6–10 min until tender. Splash in more stock if liquid reduces; drop heat to medium-low.
5. **Finish & serve.** Shred chicken with two forks while the noodles cook. Return to pot. Taste and adjust — a few dashes of fish sauce, a squeeze of lemon, or hot sauce can lift the broth. Stir in parsley and serve.

## Notes

- **Chicken thighs** are key — more flavour and stay tender. Bone-in / skin-on works too (discard skin & bones after shredding).
- **Rotisserie shortcut:** skip poaching; stir 2–3 cups shredded cooked chicken in with the noodles, reduce simmer time by 10 min.
- **Storage:** fridge 3–4 days, freezer up to 3 months. If freezing, leave noodles out and cook fresh when reheating — they soak up broth as the soup sits.
- **Extra veg:** mushrooms (with the onions), spinach/kale/peas/corn (toward the end).
