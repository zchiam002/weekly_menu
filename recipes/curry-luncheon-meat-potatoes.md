# Curry Luncheon Meat & Potatoes (Tim Ho Wan Inspired) 咖喱午餐肉马铃薯

> Cubed luncheon meat and potatoes simmered in a fragrant meat-curry sauce with cinnamon, ginger and garlic.

**Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

- 400g (14.1 oz) potatoes, cut into small cubes
- 100g (3.5 oz) red onion, chopped (or yellow onion / shallots)
- 1 pinky-length cinnamon bark
- 3 cloves garlic, grated
- ½ thumb young ginger, grated
- 600ml (20.3 fl oz) water
- ½ chicken stock cube
- 400g (14.1 oz) luncheon meat, cut into small cubes
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- Some cornstarch solution (optional)

### Curry paste
- 2½ tbsp meat curry powder
- 1 tbsp chilli powder (reduce if cooking for kids)
- Some water, to form a paste
