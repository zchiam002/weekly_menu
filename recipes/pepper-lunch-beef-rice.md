# Pepper Lunch Beef Rice 电饭煲黑胡椒牛肉饭

> Rice cooker pepper lunch — jasmine rice cooked with sweetcorn, carrots and onion, topped with shabu shabu beef and butter, finished with a coarse black pepper and salt mix.

**Servings:** 3–4

## Ingredients

### Salt & pepper mix
- 1 tbsp coarse black pepper
- 1 tsp salt

### Other
- 2 cups (270g / 9.5 oz) raw jasmine rice, rinsed
- 100g (3.5 oz) canned sweetcorn
- 120g (4.2 oz) carrots, diced
- Slightly less than 2 cups (260ml / 9.1 fl oz) water
- ½ tsp chicken stock powder
- 2 tbsp oyster sauce
- ½ tbsp premium dark soy sauce
- 1 large yellow onion, sliced
- 300g (10.6 oz) shabu shabu beef
- 20g (0.71 oz) unsalted butter
- A handful spring onions, chopped
- 1 tsp salt (or to taste)
