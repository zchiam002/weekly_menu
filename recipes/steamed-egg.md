# Steamed Egg (Ah Gong Style)

> Chinese Steamed Egg with Minced Meat — 鸡蛋蒸肉饼

**Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

### Marinated minced meat
- 150g (5.29 oz) minced pork (or chicken)
- 0.5 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp water
- 1 tbsp cornflour

### Egg mixture
- 4 eggs (~200ml or 6.76 fl oz)
- 300ml (10.14 fl oz) water (1:1.5 egg-to-water ratio)
- 0.5 tsp salt
- 0.5 tsp chicken stock powder
- 1 tsp Chinese cooking wine (Shaoxing Huatiao)

### Soy sauce mix
- 4 tbsp hot water
- 0.5 tsp sugar
- 2 tbsp light soy sauce

### Garnish
- Some chopped spring onions
