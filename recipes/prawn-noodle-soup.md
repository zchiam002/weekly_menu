# Singapore Old-School Prawn & Pork Noodles 新加坡传统排骨虾面

> Traditional Singapore prawn noodle soup with a deeply layered stock built from prawn heads, pork bones, dried shrimp, and rock sugar, finished with home-rendered lard and crispy shallot oil.

**Servings:** 3–4

## Ingredients

### Blanched pork
- 500g pork soft bones, cut into small pieces

### Soup stock
- 35g dried shrimp
- 10g white peppercorns
- 2.5L water
- 500g prawns, peeled and deveined (reserve heads and shells)
- 2 bulbs garlic
- 1 chicken stock cube
- Prawn heads and shells from the 500g prawns
- 25g rock sugar
- 3 tsp salt

### Lard & shallot oil
- 300g pork fat, cut into small bite-sized pieces
- ⅓ tsp salt
- 10 shallots, thinly sliced

### To serve
- Cooked yellow noodles (~120g per person)
- A handful of kangkong (morning glory)
- Fish cakes, sliced
