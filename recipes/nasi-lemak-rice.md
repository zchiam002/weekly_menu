# Nasi Lemak (Coconut Rice)

> The fragrant coconut-rice base for nasi lemak. Pair with sambal, ikan bilis, fried egg, cucumber, and peanuts.

**Servings:** ~4 (based on 320g dry rice)

## Ingredients

- 320g Thai jasmine rice
- 300ml water
- 220ml coconut milk
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 stalk lemongrass (use only the white stem)
- 5–6 pandan leaves
