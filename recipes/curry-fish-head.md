# Curry Fish Head 咖喱鱼头

> Spicy-tangy Nyonya-style fish head curry — a toasted dried-chilli rempah simmered with curry leaves, lemongrass, belacan and fish curry powder, brightened with tamarind and finished with coconut milk, vegetables and tofu puffs.

**Servings:** 3–4 as a main with rice, or 5–6 as part of a larger meal

## Ingredients

### Rempah
- 15 dried chillies, rehydrated and deseeded
- 20g (0.7 oz) young ginger
- 4 fresh red chillies, deseeded
- 5 cloves garlic
- 10 shallots
- 2–3 tbsp cooking oil

### Other
- 2 sprigs curry leaves
- 2 lemongrass stalks, white part only
- 10g (0.35 oz) belacan, dry toasted
- 2½ tbsp fish curry powder
- 1 litre (33.8 fl oz) water
- 1 anchovy (ikan bilis) stock cube
- Asam / tamarind water (see below)
- 1 tsp sugar
- 2 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 whole tomato, cut into wedges
- 200g (7.05 oz) aubergine (eggplant / brinjal), cut into finger-length chunks
- 100g (3.5 oz) long beans, cut into finger-length pieces
- 4 tofu puffs, cut in half
- 500–600g (17.6–21.2 oz) red snapper or grouper fish head (or fish meat)
- 5 okra (lady's fingers), cut into smaller pieces
- 150ml (5.07 fl oz) coconut milk

### Tamarind (asam) water
- 1 tbsp tamarind paste
- 200ml (6.76 fl oz) hot water
