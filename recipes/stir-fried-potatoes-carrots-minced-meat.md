# Stir-Fried Potatoes & Carrots with Minced Meat 土豆红卜炒碎肉

> Homestyle stir-fry of diced potatoes and carrots with minced meat in a savoury oyster-soy sauce.

**Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

- 300g minced meat (pork or chicken)
- ½ tsp salt
- A few dashes white pepper
- 1 medium red onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- 200g carrots, diced
- 350g potatoes, diced
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 2 tbsp light soy sauce
- 200ml water
- 2 tbsp premium dark soy sauce
- Some cornstarch solution
- Some spring onions, chopped
