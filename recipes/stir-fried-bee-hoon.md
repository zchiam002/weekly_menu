# Stir Fried Bee Hoon

> Sin Chew Beehoon 星洲米粉 / Singapore-style fried vermicelli — prawns, char siew, egg, and cabbage.

**Servings:** 4–5

## Ingredients

### Sauté prawns first
- 10 prawns

### Putting the dish together
- 1 red onion, sliced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 3 eggs, beaten
- 150g cabbage, in strips
- 250g vermicelli (Xinzhu bee hoon recommended)
- 1 tsp chicken stock powder
- 3 tsp low sodium light soy sauce
- 1.5 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 cup water
- 160g char siew (BBQ pork)
- Cooked prawns (from above)
- A few dashes of white pepper
