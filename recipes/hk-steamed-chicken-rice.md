# HK Steamed Chicken Rice (盅头饭)

> Hong Kong eatery-style self-contained steamed rice bowls — raw rice, marinated chicken wings, Chinese sausage and salted duck egg all steamed together in the bowl. Quick mid-week meal, served with chiu chow chilli oil and soy.

**Servings:** 4 bowls

## Ingredients

### The rice (per bowl — ceramic or bone china)
- 80g (2¾ oz) rice
- 100ml (⅓ cup) water
- A pinch of sugar
- A pinch of salt
- ¼ tsp vegetable oil

### The chicken wings & wind-dried Chinese meats
- 8 whole chicken wings
- 1 Chinese sausage (lap cheong)
- 2 salted (duck) eggs — from Chinese supermarkets

### The marinade
- 1 clove garlic
- 1 tsp fermented black beans
- ¼ tsp salt
- ½ tsp white pepper
- ¼ tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1 tsp pure sesame oil
- 1 tsp cornflour (cornstarch)

### To serve
- Chiu chow chilli oil
- Soy sauce
