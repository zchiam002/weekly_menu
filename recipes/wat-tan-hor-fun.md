# Wat Tan Hor Fun 滑蛋河粉

> Cantonese silky egg-gravy flat rice noodles — wok-charred kway teow finished with a prawn-head stock thickened with egg, served with sliced pork, prawns, fishcake and choy sum.

**Servings:** 2–3

## Ingredients

### Stock for gravy
- 3 slices young ginger
- 9 prawn/shrimp heads (from medium to large prawns)
- 800ml (27.05 fl oz) water
- 1 chicken stock cube
- ½ tsp salt
- ½ tsp sugar

### Marinated sliced pork
- 80g (2.82 oz) lean pork loin, thinly sliced
- A few dashes white pepper
- ½ tbsp light soy sauce
- ½ tbsp cornflour / cornstarch

### Fried flat rice noodles
- 500g (17.6 oz) flat rice noodles (kway teow)
- 1 tbsp light soy sauce
- 2 tbsp premium dark soy sauce

### Other
- 3 cloves garlic, chopped
- 9 mid-large prawns, deveined and deshelled (170g / 6 oz without shell; 270g / 9.5 oz with shell and heads)
- 90g (3.17 oz) fishcake, sliced
- A handful choy sum
- Some cornstarch solution
- 2 eggs, beaten
