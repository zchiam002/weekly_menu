# Korean Fried Chicken Wings 三味韩式炸鸡翅

> Crispy Korean-style fried chicken wings with three sauces: honey soy, black pepper, and spicy sweet.

**Servings:** Makes about 15 wings

## Ingredients

### Basic marinated wings
- 1.5kg chicken wings
- 1.5 tsp salt
- A few dashes of white pepper
- Cornflour, to coat (rest 10 mins before deep-frying)

### Honey Soy Sauce (for 5 wings)
- 1 tbsp butter
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- 2 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 tsp white vinegar (or apple cider vinegar)
- 1 tbsp honey
- Toasted sesame seeds

### Black Pepper Sauce (for 5 wings)
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 sprig curry leaves
- 1 tbsp ground black pepper
- 1 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 2 tbsp water

### Spicy Sweet Sauce (for 5 wings)
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 tbsp chilli flakes
- 2 tbsp light soy sauce
