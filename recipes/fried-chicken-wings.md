# Fried Chicken Wings

> Grandma's Famous Fried Chicken Wings — 阿嘛炸鸡翅膀 — Chinese-style crispy chicken.

**Servings:** Makes 10–11 wings (~3–4 pax)

## Ingredients

### Marinated chicken
- 1kg (35 oz) chicken wings
- 3 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1 tsp salt
- A generous amount of ground white pepper

### Batter
- 120g (4.23 oz) tapioca flour
- 40g (1.41 oz) rice flour
- 20g (0.7 oz) plain flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 1.5 tsp MSG
- A few dashes of white pepper
- 190ml (6.42 fl oz) water
