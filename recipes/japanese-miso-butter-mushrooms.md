# Japanese Miso Butter Mushrooms in Foil きのこのホイル焼き

> Assorted Japanese mushrooms wrapped in foil with miso, sake, and butter, steamed in their own juices. Serve as a side or main.

**Servings:** 4 (as side)

## Ingredients

### Seasoning
- 2 tbsp miso
- 2 tbsp sake

### Foil packets
- ½ onion (~100g)
- 4 shiitake mushrooms (~85g)
- 1 pack shimeji (brown beech) mushrooms (~100g)
- 1 pack maitake mushrooms (~100g)
- 1 pack enoki mushrooms (~85g)
- 2 king oyster mushrooms (eringi) (~113g)
- 1 tbsp unsalted butter, divided (for spreading on foil)

### To serve
- 2 tbsp unsalted butter, divided (1 tbsp per packet)
- 1 tbsp soy sauce, divided
- Chives or green onion, chopped (optional garnish)

## Method

1. **Seasoning:** Combine miso and sake in a small bowl; mix until miso fully dissolves.
2. **Prep onion:** Slice thinly (thick slices won't cook through).
3. **Prep mushrooms:**
   - Shimeji and maitake — trim bottoms, separate into small chunks.
   - Enoki — trim bottom, cut into 2-inch (5cm) pieces.
   - Shiitake — discard stems, slice thinly.
   - King oyster — trim bottom, slice thinly.
4. **Assemble packets:** Cut two sheets of foil, 14×12 inches (36×30cm). Spread butter thinly in the centre of each. Divide onion onto each sheet, then enoki on top, then pile king oyster, maitake, shimeji, and shiitake.
5. **Season:** Drizzle the miso-sake mixture over the mushrooms in each packet. Dot with remaining butter. Seal tightly so steam doesn't escape.
6. **Cook (stove):** Place packets in a frying pan, cover with a lid (no water needed). Medium heat 5 minutes, then medium-low 10–15 minutes. Open carefully to check the mushrooms are tender.
7. **Cook (oven, alternative):** Bake at preheated 425°F (218°C) for 18–20 minutes. Convection: reduce by 25°F (15°C).
8. **Serve:** Open packets hot. Top each with 1 tbsp butter, drizzle soy sauce, sprinkle chives.

### Notes
- Vegan: skip butter or use vegan butter.
- No sake: substitute broth.
- Add-ins: thinly sliced asparagus, bell pepper, zucchini, carrot, or seafood (white fish, squid, shrimp).
- Storage: airtight in fridge 2–3 days, freezer up to a month.
