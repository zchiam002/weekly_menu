# Chicken Chop & Mushroom Noodles 葱油鸡扒香菇面

> One-pan spring onion chow mein — seared marinated chicken chop served over yellow noodles stir-fried in a spring-onion-infused oil with shiitake mushrooms, red onion and a savoury dark soy sauce.

**Servings:** 3–4

## Ingredients

### Marinated chicken
- 2 whole boneless chicken legs with skin (500g / 17.64 oz), cut in half, flesh side scored
- 2 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- ½ tsp sugar
- A few dashes white pepper
- 2 cloves garlic, lightly crushed
- Some plain flour, for dry coating (optional)

### Sauce for the noodles
- 250ml (8.45 fl oz) warm water
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp chicken stock powder (optional but recommended)
- 2½ tbsp premium dark soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp light soy sauce
- A few dashes white pepper

### Spring onion-infused oil
- 5–6 tbsp cooking oil
- 4 stalks spring onion (stem part only, cut in half)

### Other
- 1 red onion, thinly sliced
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- 4 fresh shiitake mushrooms, thinly sliced
- 450g (15.87 oz) cooked yellow noodles, rinsed and drained
- A handful leafy greens
