# Salmon Kabayaki

> Japanese-style salmon pan-glazed with sweet soy. Same technique adapts to threadfin and other firm white fish.

**Servings:** 1 pax (sauce works for 2 pax — scale fish independently)

## Ingredients

- 150g (5.3 oz) salmon
- 60ml (2 oz) mirin
- 30ml (1 oz) light soy sauce
- 1 tbsp brown sugar
