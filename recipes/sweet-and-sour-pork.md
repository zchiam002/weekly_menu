# Sweet and Sour Pork

**Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

### Marinated pork
- 200g streaky pork
- 1 tbsp Shaoxing Huatiao wine
- 1 tbsp light soy sauce
- 0.5 tbsp sesame oil
- 1.5 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 egg white
- 1 tbsp cornflour
- Pepper, to taste

### Crispy coating
- Tapioca flour — enough to coat all the pork evenly

### Sweet & sour gravy
- 2 tbsp tomato sauce
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce (or black vinegar)
- 2 tbsp chilli sauce
- 1 tbsp plum sauce
- ½ cup water
- Cornstarch solution (cornstarch + water)
- 2 pieces fresh pineapple
- 1 red or green capsicum
