# Roasted Vegetables

> One-pan roasted vegetables.

**Servings:** ~4

## Ingredients

### Vegetables
- 1 medium red bell pepper (capsicum), cut into 1.5-inch squares
- 1 medium carrot, peeled and cut into long angled rustic pieces
- 1 large potato, skin on, cut into 8–10 thin wedges
- 1 large zucchini, halved then cut into six pieces
- 1 red onion, peeled and quartered
- 1 small head cauliflower, cut into florets then quartered
- 1/2 sweet potato, peeled, sliced then quartered
- 1 handful fresh basil leaves (added after roasting)

### Dressing
- 4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- 1 tsp herbs de Provence
- 0.5 tsp garlic powder
- 0.5 tsp smoked paprika
- Salt and pepper to taste
