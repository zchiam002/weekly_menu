# Prawns in Singapore Chilli Crab Sauce 新加坡风味辣椒虾

> Singapore chilli-crab flavours made easy with prawns — a sweet-savoury sambal-ketchup sauce with dried shrimp and ribbons of egg, served with fried mantou.

**Servings:** 3–4 (in a multi-dish meal)

## Ingredients

### Chilli prawns
- ½ medium red onion, finely chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
- 3g (0.1 oz) dried shrimp, rehydrated and finely chopped
- ½ tbsp sambal chilli paste
- 2 tbsp tomato ketchup
- 300ml (10.6 fl oz) water
- ½ tsp sugar
- 500g (17.6 oz) prawns, deveined
- A little cornflour slurry (cornflour dissolved in a splash of water)
- 1 egg, lightly beaten

### To serve
- Deep-fried mantou (Chinese plain buns) (optional)
