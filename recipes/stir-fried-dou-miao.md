# Stir Fried Dou Miao

> Pea shoots (豆苗) with garlic and ginger.

**Servings:** ~2–3 (as a side)

## Ingredients

### Main
- 200g pea shoots (dou miao, 豆苗)
- 2 cloves garlic
- 3 slices ginger
- 3 tbsp oil

### Seasoning
- 1 tsp oyster sauce
- 2 tsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp Chinese cooking wine
