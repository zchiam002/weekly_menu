# Fried Yam Shrimp 炸芋虾

> Dim sum fried taro prawns with crispy honeycomb texture — yam paste wrapped around whole prawn, deep-fried.

**Servings:** Makes 10

## Ingredients

### Yam paste
- 320g yam, sliced thinly
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp five spice powder
- ½ tsp baking powder
- A few dashes white pepper
- 40g vegetable shortening or lard

### Wheat starch dough
- 60g wheat starch (dim sum flour)
- 90ml hot water

### Other
- 10 large prawns, peeled & deveined, tails intact (200g without shell / 340g with shell)
