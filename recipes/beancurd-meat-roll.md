# Beancurd Meat Roll in Oyster Sauce 蚝油腐皮卷

> Dim sum steamed beancurd skin rolls with prawn + pork filling, in a light oyster-garlic sauce.

**Servings:** Yields 6 rolls

## Ingredients

### Filling
- 170g raw prawns, peeled and deveined (unpeeled weight ~300g); half roughly cut into chunks, the rest minced into a paste
- 100g minced pork or chicken
- 15g carrot, finely diced
- ½ tsp salt
- A few dashes of ground white pepper
- 1 tsp cornflour
- 3 tbsp water

### Assembly
- 1 large sheet salted beancurd skin, cut into 6 rectangles (14cm × 20cm each)
- Cornflour slurry (cornflour + splash of cold water), for sealing

### Sauce
- 3–4 garlic cloves, chopped
- 200ml water
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- ⅓ tsp chicken stock powder
- A few dashes of ground white pepper
- ½ tsp sugar
- A splash of premium dark soy sauce
- A little cornflour slurry
- 1 tsp sesame oil
