# Thai Fish Cakes (Tod Mun Pla)

> Springy, chewy Thai-style fish cakes made with tender white fish, red curry paste, and aromatic herbs — served with a fresh sweet & sour dipping sauce.

**Servings:** Not specified

## Ingredients

### Fish cakes
- Basa fillets (or sole / catfish; avoid firm fish like rockfish)
- Red curry paste (choose a brand that isn't overly salty — no extra fish sauce is added)
- Egg yolk
- Sugar (a little, to balance the curry paste's saltiness)
- Long beans, finely chopped into tiny rounds
- Kaffir lime leaves, finely slivered
- Thai basil, roughly chopped (holy basil also works)
- Oil, for shallow/pan frying

### Sweet & sour dipping sauce
- Mild red chili (large spur chili) or red bell pepper, for colour and volume
- Thai bird's eye chilies, to taste
- Garlic, whole cloves
- Cane vinegar (preferred) or white vinegar
- Sugar, a generous amount
- Water
- Salt

### Optional sauce garnishes
- Cucumber, thinly sliced
- Shallots, thinly sliced
- Peanuts, roasted and coarsely ground
