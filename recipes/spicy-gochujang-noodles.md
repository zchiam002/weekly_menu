# Spicy Korean Gochujang Noodles 韩式辣酱面

> Fast, fiery sauce-forward Korean noodles — knife-cut noodles tossed in a gochujang/gochugaru sauce with sesame paste, garlic and scallions. Sauce-based and vegetarian; easily bulked with chicken or pork.

**Servings:** 2

## Ingredients

### Noodles & base
- 6 oz dried knife-cut noodles
- ½ cup reserved noodle cooking water

### Sauce
- 2 scallions, chopped
- 2 tsp sesame seeds (divided)
- 2 tsp gochugaru (Korean chilli flakes)
- 1 tbsp gochujang (Korean chilli paste)
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 3 tbsp neutral oil
- 2 tbsp sesame paste
- 1 tbsp mirin (or sugar substitute)
- 2 tbsp light soy sauce (low sodium)
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- ¼ tsp MSG (optional)

### Optional protein
- Sliced chicken or pork, cooked through and tossed in (to make it a fuller main)
