# Egg Tofu with Shrimp 虾仁炒蛋豆腐

> Pan-fried egg tofu in a savoury oyster sauce gravy with prawns, carrot and vegetables.

**Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

- 300g egg tofu
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- 20g carrots, thinly sliced
- 160ml water
- 1.5 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp low-sodium light soy sauce
- ⅓ tsp sugar
- ⅓ tsp salt (taste the sauce first before adding)
- 100g peeled prawns
- 100g vegetables of your choice
- A few dashes white pepper
- Some cornstarch solution
