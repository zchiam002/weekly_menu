# Yang Zhou Fried Rice 扬州炒饭

> Classic Yangzhou-style fried rice with prawns, jin hua ham, eggs, and mixed vegetables. Built in three pre-fried components, then combined.

**Servings:** 4–5

## Ingredients

### Fry A — vegetables
- ½ tbsp sesame oil
- 150g cabbage, cut into cubes
- 1 cup mixed vegetables (corn, peas, carrots)

### Fry B — aromatics & prawns
- 2 tbsp sesame oil
- 3 shallots, thinly sliced
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- 120g prawns, deveined and cut into bite-sized pieces

### Final assembly
- Cooking oil
- 6 eggs
- 4 bowls cooked rice (cook 2 cups raw rice in the rice cooker, cool in the fridge before frying)
- 1 tbsp light soy sauce
- ½ tbsp oyster sauce
- ½ tsp salt (plus another ½ tsp at the end, to taste)
- Fry B (added in)
- Jin hua ham
- Fry A (added in)
- 2 stalks spring onion
- A few dashes of white pepper
