# Scallion Oil Chicken Rice 葱油鸡饭

> One-pot rice cooker chicken rice — soy-marinated chicken mid-wings steamed over jasmine rice with carrots, finished with a fragrant shallot, garlic and spring onion oil.

**Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

### Marinated wings
- 450g (12 pcs) chicken mid-wings
- 2 tbsp light soy sauce
- 2 tbsp premium dark soy sauce
- A few dashes white pepper

### Other
- 3 cups (390g) jasmine rice, washed
- 60g carrots, diced
- ½ chicken stock cube
- A few dashes white pepper
- 3 cups (450ml) water
- 1 stalk spring onion, chopped

### Fragrant shallot & spring onion oil sauce
- 3½ tbsp cooking oil
- 1 stalk spring onion stem, halved & lightly smashed
- 2 shallots, sliced
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
