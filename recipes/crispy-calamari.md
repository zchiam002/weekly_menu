# Crispy Calamari 酥脆炸鱿鱼

> Extra-crunchy fried squid rings seasoned with garlic and onion powder, in a light multi-flour batter. 10 ingredients, 10 minutes.

**Servings:** 4 (3 without fighting)

## Ingredients

- 300g (10.6 oz) squid, cleaned and cut into rings
- 1 tbsp fish sauce or light soy sauce
- 1 tbsp garlic powder
- 1 tbsp onion powder
- ¼ tsp bicarbonate of soda (baking soda)
- A few dashes ground white pepper
- 1 large egg
- 1 tbsp plain flour (all-purpose)
- 1 tbsp rice flour
- 1 tbsp potato flour or cornflour

### To coat
- Extra potato flour or cornflour, for dredging
