# Braised Chinese Mushrooms with Spinach 北菇扒菠菜

> Plump braised Chinese mushrooms in a rich abalone/oyster sauce with dried scallops, served over blanched Chinese spinach.

**Servings:** 6–8

## Ingredients

### Braised mushrooms
- 3 slices young ginger
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- 10 small–medium Chinese mushrooms, rehydrated
- ½ tbsp sesame oil
- ½ tsp sugar
- 2 tbsp abalone sauce or oyster sauce
- 15g dried scallops (soaked in 1 cup hot water & shredded)
- ½ cup water (from soaking the mushrooms)
- 2 bay leaves
- A few dashes white pepper
- Some cornstarch solution
- 1 tbsp Chinese cooking wine (Shaoxing Huatiao)

### Spinach
- 3 cloves garlic, chopped
- 250g Chinese spinach
- ½ tsp salt
