# Steamed Stuffed Tofu Puffs 蒸酿豆腐

> Tofu puffs stuffed with a seasoned minced pork filling, steamed and finished with a savoury oyster-soy sauce. No marinating needed.

**Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

### Filling (mix together)
- 250g (8.8 oz) minced pork (or minced chicken / beef)
- 1 stalk spring onion, finely chopped
- 25g (0.88 oz) carrots, finely chopped
- 2 dried shiitake mushrooms, rehydrated & chopped
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 2 tbsp light soy sauce
- ½ tsp sugar
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- A few dashes white pepper
- 2 tbsp cornflour

### To stuff
- 12 tofu puffs (65g total)

### Sauce
- 3 shallots, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- ½ cup (125ml) water
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- ¼ tsp sugar
- ½ tsp premium dark soy sauce
- Some cornstarch solution
- 1 tbsp Chinese cooking wine (Shaoxing Huatiao)
