# Potato Frittata

> Italian potato frittata — thinly sliced potatoes fried tender, bound with eggs, Parmigiano Reggiano and parsley, then cooked until golden on both sides.

**Servings:** ~4

## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 500g potatoes
- 100g Parmigiano Reggiano DOP, grated
- Parsley, as needed (finely chopped)
- Fine salt, to taste
- Black pepper, to taste
- Vegetable oil, as needed

## Method

1. **Prep potatoes:** Peel and slice the potatoes thinly (or cut into thin rounds/matchsticks) for quick, even cooking.
2. **Fry:** Heat vegetable oil in a non-stick pan and fry the potatoes over medium heat until tender and lightly golden. Season with salt. Drain off excess oil.
3. **Egg mix:** Beat the eggs in a bowl. Stir in the grated Parmigiano Reggiano, chopped parsley, salt and black pepper.
4. **Combine:** Add the cooked potatoes to the egg mixture and fold gently to coat.
5. **Cook:** Pour into a lightly oiled non-stick pan over low–medium heat. Cook undisturbed until the base is set and golden.
6. **Flip:** Slide onto a plate, invert back into the pan, and cook the other side until set and golden.
7. **Serve:** Rest briefly, then slice into wedges. Good hot or at room temperature.
