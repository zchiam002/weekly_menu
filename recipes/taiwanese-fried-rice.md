# Taiwanese Style Fried Rice

**Servings:** 4–5

## Ingredients

### Prawn prep (before pan-frying)
- 8–10 mid-size prawns, deshelled & deveined
- 1 tsp sugar
- 0.5 tsp baking soda
- Enough water to cover prawns
- Some ice cubes

### Other
- Some cooking oil
- 6 eggs
- 4 cups cooked Jasmine rice (250ml cup)
- 1.5 tsp chicken stock powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp soya sauce
- 2 stalks spring onion
- A few dashes of white pepper
