# Crispy Hakka Pork Belly 客家脆皮五花肉

> Zi char-style crunchy pork belly — thinly sliced belly marinated with fermented red tofu, lightly battered and fried until shatteringly crisp.

**Servings:** 2–3

## Ingredients

### Marinated pork belly
- 275g (9.70 oz) pork belly, skin removed and thinly sliced (weight taken before removing skin)
- 20g (0.71 oz) fermented red tofu
- ½ tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp Chinese cooking wine (Shaoxing Huatiao)
- 1 clove garlic, grated
- 25ml (0.88 fl oz) water (add more if the pork seems dry after the dry ingredients)
- 3 tbsp cornflour
- 1½ tbsp rice flour
- A pinch bicarbonate of soda (baking soda)
- A few dashes white pepper
- Some cooking oil
