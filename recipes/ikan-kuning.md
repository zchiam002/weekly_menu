# Ikan Kuning (Turmeric Fried Fish) 香脆黄尾鱼

> Crispy nasi-lemak-style turmeric fish — yellow-striped scad seasoned with turmeric and white pepper, dusted in a light flour batter and fried until shatteringly crisp. Just 6 ingredients.

**Servings:** 4–5

## Ingredients

- 1.1kg (approx. 38.8 oz) ikan kuning (yellow-striped scad), cleaned
- A few dashes white pepper
- 2 tsp turmeric powder
- 1½ tsp salt
- 3 tbsp plain flour
- 1–2 tbsp water
