# Hokkien Noodles 福建面

> KL-style dark Hokkien noodles — fresh thick noodles braised in a rich premium dark soy sauce with chicken, prawns, fishcake and choy sum.

**Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

### Marinated chicken
- 200g (7.05 oz) boneless chicken leg
- ¼ tsp salt
- A few dashes white pepper
- 1 tsp cornflour
- 1 tsp sesame oil

### Other
- 270g (9.52 oz) prawns / shrimp
- 450g (15.87 oz) fresh Hokkien noodles
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- ½ tsp sugar
- 5 tbsp premium dark soy sauce (up to 6 tbsp for darker noodles — make sure it's not the salty type)
- 1L (33.8 fl oz) water
- 1.5 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp chicken stock powder (or ¾ chicken stock cube)
- 100g (3.53 oz) fishcake
- 100g (3.53 oz) choy sum
- A few dashes white pepper
