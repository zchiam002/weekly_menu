# Prawn Crispy Noodles (Sheng Mian) 生面

> Pan-crisped wonton egg noodles topped with a prawn gravy built on a quick prawn-head stock, finished with leafy greens, egg ribbons and sesame oil.

**Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

### Crispy noodles
- 2 nests (approx. 200g / 7.1 oz) fresh wonton egg noodles (or 140g / 4.9 oz dried egg noodles)
- 1–2 tbsp cooking oil

### Gravy
- ½ large or 1 small yellow or red onion, cut into wedges
- 4 slices young ginger
- 2 spring onions, trimmed and cut into lengths
- 300g (10.6 oz) prawns (unpeeled weight); peeled and deveined, heads reserved for the stock
- 3 garlic cloves, chopped
- 500ml (17.6 fl oz) water
- ½ chicken stock cube (if using liquid chicken stock, replace the water and omit the cube)
- 1 tsp light soy sauce (plus extra to taste)
- A few dashes ground white pepper
- ¼ tsp sugar
- A handful leafy greens — kailan (Chinese broccoli) or choy sum
- A little cornflour slurry (cornflour + a splash of cold water)
- ½ or 1 egg, lightly beaten
- 1 tsp sesame oil
