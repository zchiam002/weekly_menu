# Taiwanese Braised Pork Rice 滷肉飯

> Slow-braised pork belly with shallots, dried mushrooms, and five spice in a savoury soy braise — served over rice with optional hard-boiled eggs.

**Servings:** 5–6

## Ingredients

### Pork
- 500g pork belly
- 1–2 tbsp cooking oil

### Aromatics
- 70g shallots, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, chopped
- 8 dried mushrooms, diced
- 1 tsp five spice powder
- A few dashes of white pepper

### Braising liquid
- ½ cup white rice wine
- ⅓ cup low sodium light soy sauce
- ⅓ cup premium dark soy sauce
- 15g rock sugar
- 700ml chicken stock

### Optional
- 4 hard-boiled eggs
