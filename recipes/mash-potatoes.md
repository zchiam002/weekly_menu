# Mash Potatoes

> Yukon Gold version — naturally buttery, denser and creamier finish.

**Servings:** 4–6

## Ingredients

- 2 lbs Yukon Gold potatoes, scrubbed and diced
- 1 cup milk or chicken broth (or a mix)
- 3 tbsp unsalted butter (or olive oil for a lighter version)
- 1/4 tsp fresh ground black pepper
- Salt to taste
- Optional: a splash of heavy cream or a dollop of sour cream for extra luxury
