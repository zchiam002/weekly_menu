# HK Style Steamed Fish

> CNY Perfect Cantonese Steamed Fish in Superior Soy — 港式清蒸鱼

**Servings:** 3–4 (in a multi-dish meal)

## Ingredients

### Preparing the fish
- 1 spring onion (white stem only), used as a base under the fish
- 500g (17.64 oz) whole fish (grouper, coral trout, sea bass or pomfret) — descaled, butterflied, thoroughly cleaned (no traces of blood), lightly scored
- 4–5 slices young ginger

### Sauce
- 5g (0.18 oz) young ginger, julienned
- 4 tbsp water
- 4 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1/3 tsp chicken stock powder
- 0.5 tsp sugar (or to taste — original used 1 tsp total)
- 0.5 tbsp Chinese cooking wine (Shaoxing Huatiao)

### Garnish
- Spring onions, shredded and curled
- 1–1.5 tbsp cooking oil, heated until smoking hot

## Steaming time cheat sheet (whole fish)

| Fish weight | Non-butterflied | Butterflied | Serves |
|---|---|---|---|
| 500g | 8–9 min | 6–7 min | 3–4 |
| 600g | 9–10 min | 7–8 min | 3–4 |
| 700g | 10–11 min | 8–9 min | 4 |
| 800g | 11–12 min | 9–10 min | 4 |
| 900g | 12–13 min | 10–11 min | 4–5 |
| 1.0kg | 13–14 min | 11–12 min | 4–5 |
| 1.2kg | 15–16 min | 13–14 min | 5–6 |
| 1.5kg | 18–20 min | 15–17 min | 6–7 |
| 2.0kg | 22–25 min | 18–20 min | 7–8 |
