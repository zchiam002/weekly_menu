# Watercress Pork Rib Soup 西洋菜排骨汤

> A clear, nourishing Cantonese soup — pork ribs simmered with watercress, dried scallops, red dates and wolfberries for a naturally sweet, restorative broth.

**Servings:** 5–6

## Ingredients

- 1kg pork ribs
- 500g watercress
- 30g dried scallops
- 10g wolfberries (枸杞)
- 10g red dates (红枣)
- 2 litres water
- Salt, to taste
