# Claypot Mee Tai Bak 砂煲老鼠粉

> Sizzling claypot silver needle noodles wok-fried with minced meat and a savoury soy-oyster sauce, topped with sautéed prawns, crispy lard, egg and spring onions.

**Servings:** 4–5

## Ingredients

### Sautéed prawn garnish
- 8 prawns, deveined & deshelled (leave tails intact)

### Frying the noodles
- 2 tbsp lard oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 450g minced meat
- A few dashes white pepper
- ½ tsp salt
- 1kg mee tai bak (silver needle noodles)
- 2 tbsp light soy sauce
- 2 tbsp oyster sauce
- 2 tbsp premium dark soy sauce
- ½ tsp sugar
- Some crispy lard (optional)
- 2 eggs
- Some spring onions
