# Threadfin Kabayaki

> Same technique and sauce as [salmon kabayaki](salmon-kabayaki.md) — threadfin substituted for salmon.

**Servings:** 1 pax per fillet (sauce works for 2 pax — scale fish independently)

## Ingredients

Per pax:
- ~150g threadfin fillet
- 60ml mirin (per 2 pax of sauce)
- 30ml light soy sauce (per 2 pax of sauce)
- 1 tbsp brown sugar (per 2 pax of sauce)
