# Sweet & Savoury Luncheon Meat with Potatoes

> Pan-fried potatoes and luncheon meat tossed in a quick sweet-savoury ketchup-chilli sauce.

**Servings:** 3

## Ingredients

- 3 large potatoes
- ½ can luncheon meat
- 1 clove garlic
- 3 tbsp tomato ketchup
- 1 tbsp chilli sauce
- ¼ cup water
- ½ tsp sugar
