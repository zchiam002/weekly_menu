# Scallops & Asparagus in Ginger Oyster Sauce 姜葱蚝油带子芦笋

> Seared scallops stir-fried with asparagus and carrot in a fragrant ginger oyster sauce.

**Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

- 200g scallops
- 5g young ginger, thinly sliced
- 3 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 cup water
- 50g carrots, thinly sliced
- 200g asparagus
- 2 tbsp oyster sauce
- ½ tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp light soy sauce
- 2 tbsp fresh ginger juice
- Some cornstarch solution
- 1 tsp sesame oil
