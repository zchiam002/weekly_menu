# Dauphinoise Potatoes

> Thinly sliced potatoes baked low and slow in a garlicky, nutmeg-scented cream until meltingly tender, with a browned cheesy top.

**Servings:** Large bake (scale dish to crowd)

## Ingredients

- 3kg potatoes
- 200ml milk
- 2L double cream
- 4 garlic cloves, crushed or finely grated
- Nutmeg
- 150g mild cheese
- Butter (for the dish), salt, pepper

## Method

1. Preheat the oven to 120°C.
2. Combine the cream, milk, garlic, salt, pepper and nutmeg; whisk thoroughly.
3. Peel the potatoes, then slice thinly with a sharp knife or mandolin.
4. Butter a ~20×16cm (8×6½ inch) ovenproof dish. Arrange the potato slices evenly, adding cream between the layers and a little cheese in the middle layer. Press the slices down so they're fully covered in cream.
5. Bake in a bain marie 2–3 hours, until the potato is tender and the top is nicely browned.
