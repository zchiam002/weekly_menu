# Oven-Baked Parmesan Potato Wedges

> Par-boiled potato wedges tossed with olive oil, rosemary, breadcrumbs and Parmigiano, then oven-baked until crisp and golden.

**Servings:** ~4–6 (3 lbs potatoes)

## Ingredients

- 3 lbs Yukon Gold potatoes
- ½ cup grated Parmigiano Reggiano
- ½ cup breadcrumbs
- Olive oil
- Fresh rosemary
- Salt & black pepper, to taste

## Method

1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C) and line two baking trays with parchment.
2. Cut the potatoes into wedges and boil ~6 minutes until just barely tender. Drain well.
3. In a large bowl, toss the potatoes with olive oil, salt, pepper, rosemary, breadcrumbs and Parmigiano until evenly coated.
4. Spread evenly on the trays; drizzle a little more olive oil on top.
5. Bake 1 hour, switching the trays between top and bottom racks halfway. Broil at the end for extra crisp.
