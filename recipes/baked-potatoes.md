# Baked Potatoes

> Crispy-skinned oven-baked russet potatoes — fork-poked, buttered, salted and baked directly on the rack until fluffy inside.

**Servings:** To taste (per potato)

## Ingredients

- Russet potatoes (best variety for baking)
- Butter (for a classic crust flavour; or corn / vegetable oil)
- Sea salt or kosher salt
- Toppings of choice, to serve

## Method

1. **Preheat:** Oven to 400°F (use convection if available for better airflow). Wash and dry the potatoes.
2. **Prep skin:** Poke holes all over with a fork for maximum crispiness.
3. **Coat & season:** Smother the outside generously with butter (or oil), then coat liberally with sea/kosher salt.
4. **Bake on the rack:** Place directly on a clean oven rack (not a pan) for airflow. Bake ~60 minutes.
5. **Check:** Aim for an internal temperature of 210°F.
6. **Serve:** Cut open, fluff the inside with a fork, and add your favourite toppings.
