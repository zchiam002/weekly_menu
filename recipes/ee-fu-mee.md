# Ee-fu Mee

**Servings:** 4

> Note: ee-fu noodles are not the same as yee mee.

## Ingredients

### Main
- 200g ee-fu noodles
- 1 tbsp oil
- 5 cloves garlic, minced
- Marinated dried mushrooms (see below)
- Marinated chicken (see below)
- 180g shimeji mushrooms
- 250ml chicken stock
- 1.5 tbsp oyster sauce
- 2 tbsp premium dark soya sauce
- 1 tbsp low sodium light soya sauce
- 80g Chinese chives
- 1 tbsp Chinese cooking wine (Shaoxing Huatiao)

### Marinated mushrooms
- 6 dried Chinese mushrooms, soaked and cut into strips
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 0.5 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp low sodium light soya sauce

### Marinated chicken
- 1 chicken thigh, skin removed, cut into bite size
- 1 tbsp Chinese cooking wine (Shaoxing Huatiao)
- 1 tbsp low sodium light soya sauce
- 1 tsp corn flour or tapioca flour
- A few dashes of white pepper
