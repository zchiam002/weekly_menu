# Baked Pork Chops

> Oven-baked bone-in pork chops with a smoky-sweet spice rub — paprika, garlic, onion and a touch of sugar, baked then broiled for a caramelised crust.

**Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

- 4 large bone-in pork chops (boneless works too — cook a few minutes less)
- A couple of tablespoons olive oil (to help the spices stick)
- Olive oil spray (optional — a spritz before broiling if the rub looks dry)

### Smoky sweet spice rub

- 1 tbsp paprika
- 1 tbsp coconut sugar or brown sugar
- 2 tsp garlic powder
- 2 tsp onion powder
- 1½ tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- ¼ tsp ground black pepper

## Method

1. **Mix the rub:** Combine all the dry spices in a small bowl.
2. **Prep chops:** Pat the pork chops dry, place in a baking dish, and drizzle both sides with olive oil.
3. **Season:** Rub the spice mixture generously over both sides until fully coated.
4. **Bake:** Bake at 400°F (204°C) for 15–20 min, until the thickest part reaches an internal temperature of 145°F (63°C).
5. **Optional broil for colour:** Switch to the broiler for the final few minutes (starting around 140°F internal) to caramelise the spice crust. Spritz with olive oil first if it looks dry.
6. **Rest & serve:** Rest a few minutes before serving.
