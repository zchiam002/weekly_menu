# Nasi Lemak Sambal Chilli

> The sambal that defines nasi lemak — sweet, spicy, savoury. Makes a generous batch; keeps well refrigerated.

**Yield:** ~1 batch (covers many nasi lemak servings)

## Ingredients

### (A) Blend into a fine paste
- 25 dried chillies, rehydrated
- 1 stalk lemongrass (use only the white stem)
- 1 whole red onion
- 8 shallots
- 4 cloves garlic
- 20g dried shrimps, rehydrated
- 10g dried anchovies (dry-fried to crispy)
- Some cooking oil

### (B) Other
- ½ cup (125ml) oil
- 20g belacan, dry-roasted
- 1 tsp tamarind paste
- 40g palm sugar (or white / brown sugar)
- ⅔ cup (170ml) dried shrimp soaking liquid (reserved from rehydrating)
