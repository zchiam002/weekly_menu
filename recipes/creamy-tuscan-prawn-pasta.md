# Creamy Tuscan Prawn Pasta

> Prawns in a rich creamy Tuscan-style sauce with sun-dried and cherry tomatoes, baby spinach, garlic and Parmesan, tossed through pasta.

**Servings:** Not specified in source (makes enough sauce for ~3–4 with pasta)

## Ingredients

- 300ml Bulla thickened cream
- 16 large prawns (600g with shell / 320g without), deshelled and deveined
- 100g baby spinach
- 1 yellow onion, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, chopped
- 50g sun-dried tomatoes, coarsely chopped
- 100g cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
- 1½ tsp Italian herbs
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 20g unsalted butter
- Cooked pasta, as needed
- Chopped parsley, to garnish
