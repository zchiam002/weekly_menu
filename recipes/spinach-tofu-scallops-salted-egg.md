# Spinach Tofu & Crispy Scallops in Salted Egg Yolk Sauce 金玉满堂

> A showpiece dish — homemade spinach egg tofu topped with crispy battered scallops and enoki mushrooms, all coated in a golden salted egg yolk sauce with curry leaves and butter.

**Servings:** 8

## Ingredients

### Egg tofu
- 120g spinach
- 8 eggs
- 600ml fresh unsweetened soya bean milk
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp Knorr SavorRich chicken concentrated seasoning

### Crispy scallops
- 12 large scallops
- 90g self-raising flour
- 1 tsp margarine
- 90ml water
- A pinch of salt

### Crispy enoki mushrooms
- 30g enoki mushrooms
- 2 tbsp cornflour
- 2 tbsp water

### Golden salted egg yolk sauce
- Some cooking oil
- 40g unsalted butter
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- 2 bird's eye chillies, chopped (optional)
- A handful of curry leaves
- 3 tbsp Knorr Salted Egg Powder
- 160ml evaporated milk
- 40ml water
- 1.5 tsp sugar
- 1.5 tsp salt
