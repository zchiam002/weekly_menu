# Seafood Pao Fan 海鲜泡饭

> Hong Kong-style restaurant soup rice — a rich, cloudy broth built from chicken feet, prawn heads, dried sole fish and ikan bilis, poured over rice with crispy rice, crispy egg, crispy whitebait and garlic oil on top.

**Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

### Broth
- 500g (17.64 oz) chicken feet
- 40g (1.41 oz) dried anchovies (ikan bilis)
- 1 tbsp white peppercorns
- 2 dried sole fish / flounder fish (30g / 1.06 oz)
- 400g (14.1 oz) prawn heads
- 250ml (8.45 fl oz) water (for frying the prawn heads)
- 1.5L (50.72 fl oz) water
- 1 bulb garlic, washed (skin on)
- 1 thumb-sized young ginger, lightly crushed
- 3g rock sugar (or slightly less than 1 tsp white sugar)
- 1 tsp salt (or to taste)

### Other
- Cooked rice for 4 (about 3 cups dry rice)
- ½ bowl cooked rice (to make crispy rice)
- 2 eggs, beaten (to make crispy egg)
- 20g (0.71 oz) whitebait fish or dried anchovies (to make crispy whitebait)
- Garlic oil (½ bulb chopped garlic fried in oil)
