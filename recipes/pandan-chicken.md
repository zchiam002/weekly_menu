# Pandan Chicken

> Thai-style marinated chicken thigh wrapped in pandan leaves — lemongrass, ginger, and coconut milk in the marinade.

**Servings:** Makes 16 pandan chicken

## Ingredients

### Marinade aromatics
- 2 lemongrass stalks (white part only)
- 3 garlic cloves
- 3 shallots
- 12g young ginger
- 2 coriander roots

### Marinade seasoning
- 15g palm sugar (or brown / white sugar)
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 2 tbsp fish sauce (or light soy sauce)
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 2 tbsp coconut milk

### Chicken
- 420g chicken thigh (or chicken breast / fillet)
