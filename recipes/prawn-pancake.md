# Prawn Pancake

> Prawn and beancurd skin pancake — chopped prawns folded with carrot and coriander, wrapped in beancurd skin.

**Servings:** 8

## Ingredients

- 350g prawns (18 pieces)
- 1 piece beancurd skin (wipe clean, cut into 4 pieces)
- ⅓ carrot, chopped
- 1 tsp cornflour (or tapioca flour)
- 2–3 stalks Chinese coriander
- 1 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1 egg white (from 1 egg)
