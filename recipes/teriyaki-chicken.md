# Teriyaki Chicken 照り焼きチキン

> Authentic Japanese pan-fried chicken thigh glazed with a simple sake-mirin-soy teriyaki sauce.

**Servings:** 1–2 (scale up per chicken thigh)

## Ingredients

### Chicken
- 1 chicken thigh (bone-in or boneless)

### Teriyaki sauce
- 35g koikuchi soy sauce
- 30g sake
- 30g mirin
- 15g sugar

## Method

1. **Make the sauce:** Combine soy sauce, sake, mirin, and sugar in a small bowl. Stir to dissolve the sugar.
2. **Prep the chicken:** Score the thicker parts of the thigh and trim excess fat or sinew so it cooks evenly.
3. **Fry the chicken:** Place skin-side down in a cold pan, then turn on medium heat. Render the fat slowly until the skin is deep golden and crisp. Flip and cook the flesh side through.
4. **Glaze:** Pour off excess oil. Add the teriyaki sauce. Tilt and baste the chicken as the sauce reduces and thickens into a glossy glaze, coating both sides.
5. **Rest and slice:** Transfer to a board, rest briefly, then slice across the grain. Spoon remaining glaze over the top.
