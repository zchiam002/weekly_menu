# Mee Hoon Kway 面粉粿

> Hand-torn flour-dough noodles in a deep ikan bilis and chicken-feet broth, with pork-and-fish-paste meatballs. Same base works for ban mian (just pull noodles into strips instead of tearing). Add spinach to the bowl to make it spinach ban mian.

**Servings:** 10–12

## Ingredients

### Noodle dough
Mix together into a dough; rest in the fridge for 45 minutes to 1 hour.

- 1kg plain flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp cooking oil
- 2 eggs
- 0.5L water
- Extra plain flour, for dusting

### Soup base
Combine in a pot; bring to a boil on high, then reduce to low and simmer 1 hour.

- 6L water
- 750g chicken feet, blanched
- 300g anchovies (ikan bilis)
- 250g soya bean
- 2 tbsp white peppercorns
- 1 thumb-sized ginger, crushed

### Minced meat
Mix everything together; chill in the fridge until ready to use in the soup.

- 1kg minced pork
- 600g fish paste
- 2 tbsp flounder fish powder (or ikan bilis powder / chicken powder)
- 2 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1½ tbsp cornstarch or tapioca flour
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp salt
- A generous dash of pepper

### Bowl additions (per serve)
- Prawns
- Sliced fish
- Wolfberry leaves
- Spinach (use this for spinach ban mian)
