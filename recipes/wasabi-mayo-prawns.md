# Wasabi Mayo Prawns

**Servings:** 3

## Ingredients

### Basic mayonnaise (from scratch)
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tbsp mustard
- 200ml sunflower seed oil
- 0.5 tsp salt
- A few dashes of white pepper
- 1 tbsp vinegar
- 1 tbsp lemon juice

### Wasabi mayonnaise
- 1 tbsp basic mayonnaise
- 1–2 tsp wasabi

### Orange mayonnaise
- 1 tbsp basic mayonnaise
- 1–2 tsp orange marmalade

### Prawns
- 9 medium to large prawns (deshelled & deveined)
- 0.5 tsp salt
- A generous dash of white pepper
- 1 egg white
- Some cornflour
