# Taiwanese Pork Chop

**Servings:** 3

## Ingredients

- 500g (17.64 oz) boneless pork loin, cut into three pieces
- 50ml (1.69 fl oz) water
- 3 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- A few dashes of white pepper
- 1 tsp five spice powder
- 2 cloves garlic, lightly smashed
- 0.5 tsp baking soda
- 2 tbsp cornflour or cornstarch
