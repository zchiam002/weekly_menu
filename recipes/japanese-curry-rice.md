# Japanese Curry Rice

> Homemade Japanese curry — chicken, potato, and carrot in a rich roux-based sauce. No packet roux.

**Servings:** 6

## Ingredients

- 200g yellow onion, finely diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 15g ginger, minced
- 35g butter
- 60g plain flour
- 40g meat curry powder
- 1 heaping tsp chilli powder
- 1.5 tsp chicken stock powder
- 4 cups water
- 2 tbsp tomato puree
- 1.5 tsp sugar
- 3 tbsp premium Japanese light soy sauce
- 400g potatoes, cut into cubes
- 140g carrots, cut into cubes
- 2 chicken thighs, cut into cubes
