# Thai Style Steamed Fish 泰式蒸鱼

> Whole sea bass steamed over cabbage and kaffir lime leaves, drenched in a punchy blended sauce of lime, garlic, Thai chilli, cilantro root and fish sauce.

**Servings:** 3–4 (700g fish, in a multi-dish meal)

## Ingredients

### Sauce (blend together)
- Lime juice (青柠汁) — 5 tbsp
- Garlic (蒜头) — 50g
- Thai chillies (泰国辣椒) — 10 pcs *(reduce for less heat)*
- Cilantro roots (芫荽根) — 3 pcs
- Sugar (糖) — 3½ tbsp
- Fish sauce (鱼露) — 4 tbsp

### Fish
- Sea bass (石甲鱼) — 700g
- Cabbage (包菜) — 30g (as a bed under the fish)
- Kaffir lime leaves (泰国柠檬叶) — 2 pcs

### Garnish
- Lime slices and cilantro (青柠片和芫荽)
