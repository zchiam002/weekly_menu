# Ngoh Hiang Meat Rolls 五香肉卷

> Classic Hokkien five-spice rolls — a prawn and minced pork filling with water chestnuts and onion, wrapped in beancurd skin and fried.

**Servings:** Makes 7 rolls

## Ingredients

- 500g prawns (shell-on weight; ~280g without shells)
- 500g minced pork
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 4–5 water chestnuts, chopped
- 1 heaped tbsp five spice powder
- 1 tsp chicken stock powder
- ¾ tsp salt (up to 1 tsp)
- A few dashes white pepper
- 2 tbsp plain flour
- 1 big piece dried beancurd skin, cut into 20cm × 20cm pieces (about 7)
