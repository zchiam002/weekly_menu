# Oven Baked Chicken Leg

> Easy oven-baked chicken leg quarters — marinated with lemon pepper, smoked paprika, oregano and ginger-garlic, then roasted until golden and juicy.

**Servings:** ~6

## Ingredients

- 6 chicken leg quarters (or your favourite cut)
- 1 tbsp lemon pepper seasoning (contains salt — no extra salt needed)
- 1 tbsp all-purpose seasoning (or 1 chicken bouillon cube)
- ½ tbsp smoked paprika
- ½ tbsp dry or fresh oregano
- A pinch of cumin (~1/6 tsp)
- 1 tbsp half-and-half ginger & garlic paste
- 1 tbsp melted butter (or olive oil)

## Method

1. **Prep:** Pat the chicken dry. Combine the lemon pepper, all-purpose seasoning, smoked paprika, oregano, cumin and ginger-garlic paste with the melted butter into a marinade.
2. **Marinate:** Rub all over the chicken, getting under the skin where possible. Marinate at least 30 min (or overnight in the fridge for deeper flavour).
3. **Bake:** Arrange on a lined tray and roast at 200°C (400°F) until the skin is golden and the juices run clear / internal temp reaches 74°C (165°F), about 40–45 min depending on size.
4. **Rest & serve:** Rest a few minutes before serving.
