# Hainanese Chap Chye 海南杂菜

> Comforting braised mixed vegetables — cabbage and carrot simmered with dried shrimp, Chinese mushrooms, bean curd skin and glass vermicelli in a savoury stock.

**Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

- 6 cloves garlic, chopped
- 15g dried shrimp, rehydrated
- 4 dried Chinese mushrooms, rehydrated & sliced
- 320g round cabbage
- 100g carrot, thinly sliced
- 1 cup water (used to soak the dried shrimp)
- 2 cups water (used to soak the dried mushrooms)
- 1 tbsp concentrated chicken stock (or 1 tsp chicken stock powder)
- 50g dried bean curd skin
- 50g glass vermicelli (tanghoon)
- ¼ tsp sugar
- ½ tsp salt (or to taste)
