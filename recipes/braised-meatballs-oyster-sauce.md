# Braised Meatballs in Oyster Sauce 蚝油焖肉丸

> Bouncy prawn-and-pork meatballs studded with carrot, browned then braised in a savoury oyster and dark soy sauce with shallots, ginger and garlic.

**Servings:** 3–4

## Ingredients

### Meatballs
- 150g (5.29 oz) prawns
- 150g (5.29 oz) minced pork (or minced chicken)
- 1 egg white
- 1 tsp fresh young ginger juice
- 20g (0.7 oz) diced carrots
- ½ tsp salt
- A few dashes ground white pepper
- 1.5 tbsp cornflour

### Sauce
- 3 shallots, chopped
- 8g (0.28 oz) young ginger
- 3 cloves garlic, chopped
- 250ml (8.45 fl oz) water
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp premium dark soy sauce
- A pinch of sugar
