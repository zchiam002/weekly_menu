# Spring Onion Beef Stir Fry (Ginger & Spring Onion) 姜葱牛肉

> Tender velveted beef stir-fried with plenty of young ginger, onion and spring onion in a savoury soy-oyster sauce.

**Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

### Marinated beef
- 280g chuck tender, thinly sliced against the grain
- 2 tsp cornflour
- 1 tbsp cooking oil
- ½ tsp baking soda
- 2 tbsp water
- A few dashes pepper

### The rest
- 7–8 slices young ginger
- 2 onions, thinly sliced
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
- 2 tbsp light soy sauce (low sodium)
- 4 stalks spring onion
- ½ cup water
- 2 tbsp Chinese cooking wine (Shaoxing Huatiao)
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
