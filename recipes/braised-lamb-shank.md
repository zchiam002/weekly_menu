# Braised Lamb Shank 焖羊腿

> One-pot braised boneless lamb leg with onion, garlic, tomato, red wine and root vegetables — pressure-cooker / multi-cooker friendly.

**Servings:** 4–5

## Ingredients

- 900g (31.75 oz) boneless lamb leg, cut into ~5cm pieces
- 1 tsp salt
- 1½ tsp black pepper
- 1 medium red or yellow onion, chopped
- 6 cloves garlic, lightly smashed
- 4 tbsp tomato purée / paste (recommended to reduce to 3 tbsp)
- 250ml (8.80 fl oz) red wine
- 2 bay leaves
- 270g (9.52 oz) carrots
- 450g (15.87 oz) potatoes
- 1L (35.20 fl oz) beef stock
