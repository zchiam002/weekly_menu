# Thai Holy Basil Stir-Fry (Pad Kra Pao)

> Spicy Thai stir-fry of ground chicken with holy basil, chilies, and garlic — served over jasmine rice with a crispy fried egg (Kai Dow) and prik nam pla on the side.

**Servings:** Not specified

## Ingredients

### Aromatics & chili paste
- Thai bird's eye chilies, for heat
- Mild red chilies (prik cheefa / spur chilies), roughly chopped, for colour and flavour
- Garlic, plenty of whole cloves

### Protein & vegetables
- Coarsely ground chicken (Pailin uses a mix of thigh and breast; ground pork or bite-sized meat also works)
- Holy basil (kaprow), generous handful of fresh leaves (Thai basil as substitute)
- Long beans, cut into short pieces (or regular green beans)
- Onions, diced

### Stir-fry sauce ("Trinity of Thai Stir-Fry")
- Oyster sauce
- Soy sauce
- Fish sauce
- Dark soy sauce, a splash for colour
- Sugar, to balance saltiness
- Water, to loosen the sauce

### For serving (Thai style)
- Jasmine rice, cooked
- Eggs, for crispy fried egg (Kai Dow)
- Vegetable oil (high smoke point), for deep-frying the egg and stir-frying
- Prik nam pla: fish sauce with fresh chilies, garlic, and lime juice
