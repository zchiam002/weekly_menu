# Beef Stew

> Roland's classic braised beef stew — beef chuck tender in red wine + tomato base with carrot and potato.

**Servings:** 4–6

## Ingredients

### Beef
- 520g beef chuck tender, cut into 2.5cm (1 inch) cubes
- 10–20g plain flour, for dusting

### Stew base
- 1 large yellow onion, finely diced
- 4 garlic cloves, chopped
- 200ml red wine
- 4 tbsp tomato paste
- 1½ tsp ground black pepper
- 2 tsp mixed Italian herbs
- 750ml beef broth
- 2 bay leaves
- 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1½ tsp salt

### Vegetables
- 300g carrots, cut into chunks
- 500g potatoes, cut into chunks
