# Tamagoyaki

> Japanese rolled omelette.

**Servings:** ~2 as a side (1 roll from 4 eggs)

## Ingredients

- 4 large eggs
- Salt, to taste
- Soy sauce, to taste
- Mirin (or sugar), to taste
- Cooking oil — to grease the pan between rolling each layer
