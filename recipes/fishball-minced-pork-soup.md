# Fishball Minced Pork Soup

> Simple, clear chicken-broth soup with fish balls and soy-seasoned minced pork. Blanched leafy greens (e.g. spinach) can be added at the end for variation.

**Servings:** 4–6

## Ingredients

### Soup
- Chicken broth (enough for the household)
- Anchovy powder, to taste
- Some cooking oil
- Fish balls

### Minced pork
- Minced pork
- Light soy sauce
- White pepper

### Optional add-in
- Spinach or other leafy greens, blanched separately and added at serving

## Method

1. Heat a little oil in a pot. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a simmer.
2. Add anchovy powder to taste and adjust seasoning.
3. Season the minced pork with light soy sauce and white pepper; drop small portions into the simmering broth so they form loose meatballs as they cook.
4. Add the fish balls and simmer until heated through and the pork is cooked.
5. (Optional) Blanch spinach in boiling water briefly, drain, and add to the bowls just before serving.
