# Chinese Chicken & Vegetable Stir Fry 鸡肉炒杂菜

> A homestyle chicken stir-fry with French beans, carrot, baby corn, button mushrooms and capsicum in a savoury oyster-soy sauce.

**Servings:** 4–5

## Ingredients

### Marinated chicken
- 1 chicken thigh, cut into strips, skin removed
- 2 tsp cornflour
- ½ tsp salt
- A few dashes white pepper
- ½ tbsp cooking oil

### Other (add in sequence)
- 3 slices ginger
- 5 cloves garlic, chopped
- ½ cup water
- 100g French beans
- ½ carrot, sliced
- ¼ cup water
- Marinated chicken (above)
- ½ cup water
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp light soy sauce
- ½ cup water
- 10 pieces baby corn
- 1 can button mushrooms
- ½ red capsicum, sliced
- 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
- 1 tbsp Chinese cooking wine (Shaoxing Huatiao)
- A few dashes white pepper
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
