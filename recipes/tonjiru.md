# Tonjiru 豚汁

> Hearty Japanese pork miso soup — pork belly simmered with daikon, carrot, potato, onion and burdock root (gobo) in a rich white miso broth.

**Servings:** 6

## Ingredients

- 200g (7.05 oz) pork belly, thinly sliced
- 1 large yellow onion, cut into wedges
- 100g (3.53 oz) carrots, thinly sliced
- 200g (7.05 oz) daikon radish, sliced (slightly thicker than carrots)
- 2L (67.63 fl oz) water (or chicken stock or dashi)
- 2 chicken stock cubes (omit if using dashi or chicken stock)
- 300g (10.58 oz) potatoes, cut into chunks
- 100g (3.53 oz) gobo (burdock root), sliced — can omit if unavailable
- 120g (4.23 oz) white miso (start with 1 tbsp, adjust to taste)
- Spring onions, chopped (to serve)
