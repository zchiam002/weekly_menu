# Chicken Chop

> Singapore / Malaysian Style Chicken Chop — 西式鸡扒

**Servings:** 3

## Ingredients

### Marinated chicken
- 3 boneless chicken legs, butterflied (~220g / 7.76 oz each)
- 3 tbsp light soy sauce
- 2 tbsp oyster sauce
- 0.5 tsp sugar
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- A few dashes of white pepper

### Gravy
- 20g (0.7 oz) unsalted butter
- 1 tsp ground black pepper
- 1/3 chicken stock cube
- 8g (0.5 tbsp) all-purpose flour
- 200ml (6.76 fl oz) water
- 0.5 tsp dark soy sauce

### Sides
- Some french fries
- Some baked beans
- Some mixed vegetables
- Some dinner rolls or bread
