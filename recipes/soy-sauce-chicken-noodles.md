# Soy Sauce Chicken Noodles 豉油鸡面

> Cantonese soy sauce chicken (boneless leg poached in a fragrant master sauce of light/dark soy, rock sugar, star anise and cinnamon) served over wonton noodles with leafy greens.

**Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

### Marinated chicken
- 2 pieces boneless chicken leg (about 480g total)
- 2 tbsp light soy sauce
- 2 tbsp premium dark soy sauce

### Sauce
- 800ml (27.05 fl oz) water
- 2 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 60ml (2.03 fl oz) premium dark soy sauce
- 4 cloves garlic (lightly smashed)
- ½ piece star anise
- ½ piece cinnamon bark
- 1 stalk spring onion (tied into a knot)
- 1 thumb-sized young ginger (lightly smashed)
- 20g (0.71 oz) rock sugar
- Some cornstarch solution

### Other
- Some leafy vegetables
- Wonton noodles (about 70g / 2.47 oz per person)
