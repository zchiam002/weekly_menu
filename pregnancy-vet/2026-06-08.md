## Wednesday — Taiwanese braised pork rice 滷肉飯
Verdict: CAUTION
Notes:
- Pork belly braised long → fully cooked (Western).
- ½ cup white rice / Shaoxing wine in the braising liquid → this is a large amount. Long braising drives off most of the alcohol but not all; at this volume a residual trace can remain, which matters in T1 where the guidance is effectively zero alcohol (Western).
- Optional hard-boiled eggs → fully cooked, fine.
- Five-spice / white pepper → mildly warming; fine in T1 (TCM).
Suggested adjustment: cut the wine right back to a token splash, or replace it with extra chicken stock (plus a little more dark soy for colour). Make sure the braise stays at a proper simmer, uncovered enough to cook the alcohol off.

  - Lemongrass — leave it out of the green curry this week (TCM pregnancy caution).
